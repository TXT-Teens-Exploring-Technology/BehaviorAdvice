Physical Aggression

Why this happens?

They are angry and want to hurt someone who has upset them, or they want attention.

Children witnesssed hitting and they are mimicking behavior.

They are responding physically because they dont know how to verbalize their feelings.

Strategies

1

Children need to know what behavior is, and is not, permitted. Make sure your child is aware of the rules you set and punishments.



2

Encurage them to use words to express their feelings rather than fighting. calmly ask your child to explain what has caused them to become so angry.



3

Children dont possess an innate ability to control themselves. They need to be taught not to kick, hit, or bite wether they feel like i.
